Interests

I love hiking, trekking, and exploring the great outdoors. There's something truly special about being in nature and experiencing its beauty firsthand. I make sure every weekend I do something outdoorsy, whether it's a long hike or a short nature walk. I want to visit all the famous hiking trails in the world, and I have a long list of places I want to explore. Some of my favorite nature spots yet include the Angels's landing in Zion National Park, Old man of Storr in Scotland, and the Kheerganga trek in the Himalayas.

I got introduced to quizzing by my roommates at BITS and have enjoyed it since. Participating in quiz leagues and trivia nights is always a fun challenge for me. I enjoy the thrill of competing and the joy of learning new facts. Some of my favorite quiz topics include business, science, and pop culture.

Food has a special place in my heart. I enjoy experimenting with new recipes and exploring different cuisines. Cooking is a creative outlet for me, and I love sharing my culinary creations with friends and family. Some of my favorite dishes to make include the staple dal-chawal, chicken tikka, pasta, and traditional Indian curries.
